I saw this animation from [NVIDIA's 2022 GTC presentation](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Uo8rs5YfIYY&t=405s) and I thought it was neat.  So, I set out to re-create it.

[You can see it here](https://squarism.com/nvidia_sorter/).

|Keys||
|:-|:-|
|?| this keys list|
|g| the GPU overlay that the presentation fades in and out|
|space | pause, at least the belt movement |
|d| debug: destination markers|
|w| debug: wireframe of "the belt"|

[Source is here](https://github.com/squarism/nvidia_sorter).
