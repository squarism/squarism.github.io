PuppetLabs Spec

Qualifications

  • You have knowledge and experience (3-5 years) of high-level programming languages like ruby, python, java, etc.
  • You have developed and implemented solutions to challenging automation problems.
  • You think web APIs to control hardware and infrastructure are brilliant.
  • You are a believer in thoroughly testing software, automate that testing and believe that better QA drives better quality. You have experience with test automation frameworks and continuous integration.
  • Being an active member of open source communities is something you cherish, and you have experience helping community members advance their code.
  • You are not dogmatic about technology choices. You understand that what matters is using the right tool for the job at hand.
  • You understand and use distributed version control systems such as git.
  • Minimum 3 years of full time experience in a software development role (5+ years preferred).
  • BSCS or equivalent or relevant industry experience.
  • Experience in writing high-quality, reusable Puppet modules is a plus.
  • Requires logic and reasoning to identify the strengths and weaknesses of alternative solutions, conclusions or approaches to problems.
  • Requires contact with others (face-to-face, by telephone, virtual meetings, VOIP or otherwise) and the ability to work and interact in a group or team.
